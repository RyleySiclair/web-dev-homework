Sugar Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vamilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Stir together flour, baking soda, and baking powder.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream together buter and sugar until smooth.
  4. Next beat in the egg and vanilla.
  5. Gradually mix in the dry ingredients.
  6. Scoop a teaspoon of dough and make into balls, then place on ungreased cookie sheet.
  7. Bake for 8-10 min or until golden.
  8. Let the cookies rest for 2 min before removing from sheet.
  9. Enjoy!
