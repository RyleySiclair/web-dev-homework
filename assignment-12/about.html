About Page

We are a local Bookstore located in Spokane, Washington. We want to get more people involved with reading and writing, so we created the perfect atmosphere to do so. Come in and experiance first hand the selection of reading and writing materials that we offer, as well as a small town cafe with drinks and beverages of your choice. This is the perfect place to be! So many options and so much inspiration all under one roof. Our friendly staff welcomes you to bring you and all your friends to R&R Book! All are welcome, including furry four legged friends:)!
